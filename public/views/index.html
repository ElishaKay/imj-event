Dear Friends,

We are delighted to invite you to join us for the 2020 International Council, which will take place this year from Sunday evening, June 7, through Wednesday, June 10.

This year’s Council will celebrate the very special 55th Anniversary of the Museum, when we will share together the Museum’s past and present achievements, as well as our vision and aspirations for the future.

Opening with our traditional poolside reception on Sunday evening, hosted by the Federmann family at the King David Hotel, our rich program of activities and events will include curator-led tours of current exhibitions; hands-on workshops highlighting important recent acquisitions; a festive Gala Dinner; meetings with guest artists; talks and discussions with eminent guest speakers; our traditional surprise venue dinner under the stars; and much more. We will also continue to offer behind-the-scenes glimpses into the inner workings of the Museum that have proved so popular with Council participants in recent years, as well as sneak previews into our 2021 exhibition program. And of course there will be ample time for you to connect with friends from around the globe, both old and new, and to meet with the Museum’s management, curators, and professional staff.

We look forward to welcoming you in June to Jerusalem and to the Museum for what promises to be an exciting, enriching, and celebratory International Council!

With warmest regards,

Maureen Cogan
